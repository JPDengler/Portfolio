Hello, I'm Joseph Dengler, an Automation Technician with a background in software development and industrial automation. I work at Bridor USA, where I troubleshoot and maintain PLC systems (Siemens & Allen-Bradley), servo drives, safety relays, sensors, and electrical cabinets. My role involves logic programming, electrical diagnostics, and process optimization, ensuring seamless operation in a high-demand production environment. I am also developing an admin dashboard website for our maintenance department to streamline access to documentation, inventory, and work history.

I am completing my Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science with a concentration in Software Engineering at Southern New Hampshire University, graduating March 1st, 2025. Throughout my studies, I have maintained a 4.0 GPA, earning distinctions such as Alpha Sigma Lambda, Dean’s List, President’s List, and Honor Roll. My academic experience has strengthened my problem-solving skills, bridging software development and automation technology.

My technical expertise spans C++, Java, JavaScript, Python, and MySQL, with experience in logic programming, embedded systems, and industrial automation scripting. Beyond my professional work, I have contributed to various software and hardware projects, including Project Magpie (this portfolio), mock app prototypes, and IoT projects like Project BMO, a voice-interactive robot using Raspberry Pi and OpenAI.

Previously, I worked as a Quality Assurance Technician at Radwell International, troubleshooting and repairing electronic systems primarily Servo Drives. This role honed my analytical skills and reinforced my ability to work in fast-paced, technical problem-solving environments.

I am passionate about automation, software solutions, and emerging tech. Whether optimizing control systems, integrating software-driven automation, or improving user experiences through web and embedded applications, I enjoy tackling complex challenges that enhance efficiency and innovation.
